Concept

Our game is a cooperative non-euclidean-looking puzzle game. It will be played by two players, who will have to advance in parallel in two similar mazes, cooperate as well as share information in order to progress through the rooms.

History

Hyper Pursuit is a puzzle game created in the scope of a school project. We wanted to create a game in a weird world. In fact, the first concept of the game was creating either a simple maze in augmented reality or a lucrative programming game, but the final choice was narrowed down to creating an advanced version of a maze inspired from Antichamber, Portal and many others. The game is in constant evolution since its creation, additionally, some new scenes and levels will continuously be released.
